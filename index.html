Research

The circumgalactic medium (CGM) is a key region where galaxy evolution happens. It is a multiphase region where we can observe galactic inflows and outflows. These are directly related to the stellar feedback of the galaxies. Our groups goal is to better characterize the CGM and to understand the key factors which fuel its processes. My current work focuses on using Hubble Space Telescope data to better understand the warm (log T ~ 5.5-6.0 K) phase of the CGM.
